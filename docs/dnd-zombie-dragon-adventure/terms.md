# DnD Zombie Dragon Adventure Terms

## Build

The overall combination of gear, stat choices, and rewards used during a run.

## Early Areas

Lower-risk zones used to build gold and progress before pushing farther.

## Equipment Store

The upgrade menu where players spend gold to improve gear slots.

## Reward Choice

A room-clear reward screen that lets the player choose one item from several options.

## Tower

A stronger area players described as more useful once survivability is good enough.
