# DnD Zombie Dragon Adventure Tasks

## Following a Safer Early Progression Loop

Use this route if you are stuck early or unsure what to do next.

![Forest A enemies left](/img/dnd-zombie-dragon-adventure/areas/dnd-area-forest-a-enemies-left.jpg)

*Forest A screenshot showing an early area where players can build strength before pushing farther.*

### Steps

1. Farm the beginning areas first.
2. Stay there until you can clear rooms consistently.
3. Use successful clears to build up gold.
4. Move into stronger zones only after early runs feel reliable.

### Important

- Early farming is slower, but more stable
- Forcing harder areas too soon usually wastes time

---

## Repeating the Gear Upgrade Loop

Use gold from repeated clears to improve your gear over time.

![Equipment store upgrades](/img/dnd-zombie-dragon-adventure/gear/dnd-gear-equipment-store-upgrades.jpg)

*Equipment Store screen showing upgrade paths for head, chest, shoulder, gloves, pants, and boots.*

### Steps

1. Clear rooms in lower-risk areas.
2. Save the gold from those clears.
3. Visit the **Equipment Store**.
4. Upgrade the gear slots you can afford.
5. Return to farming and repeat.

### Important

- Gear upgrades appear to be one of the main long-term power ramps
- Skipping upgrades will likely slow progression

---

## Using Room Rewards Better

When a room gives you a reward choice, use it to support your current build.

![Valley A choose-one reward](/img/dnd-zombie-dragon-adventure/rewards/dnd-reward-valley-a-choose-one.jpg)

*Reward screen after clearing a room in Valley A.*

### Steps

1. Clear the room.
2. Review each reward option.
3. Compare the stat bonuses.
4. Pick the item that best supports your build or covers a weakness.
5. Keep doing this as your run develops.

### Important

- Not every item is equally useful in every run
- Smart reward choices can matter more than random picks

---

## Moving Into Stronger Areas

Push into stronger zones only after your farming route and upgrades are working.

![Tower E room cleared](/img/dnd-zombie-dragon-adventure/areas/dnd-area-tower-e-room-cleared.png)

*Tower E screenshot showing a cleared room in a stronger area.*

### Steps

1. Farm early areas until clears are steady.
2. Upgrade gear between runs.
3. Use reward choices to strengthen your build.
4. Move into stronger zones after your runs become reliable.
5. Repeat the same loop there if the zone becomes sustainable.

### Important

- Stronger areas seem more useful once you are ready for them
- Entering them too early can break the whole loop

---

## Checking Run Results

Use run-end results to judge whether your route is working.

![Victory rewards screen](/img/dnd-zombie-dragon-adventure/results/dnd-result-victory-rewards.jpg)

*Victory screen showing gold earned, survival time, and rooms cleared during a run.*

### Steps

1. Finish a run.
2. Check how much gold you earned.
3. Check how long you survived.
4. Check how many rooms you cleared.
5. Decide whether to repeat the same route or return to an easier one.

### Important

- A stable run is usually more valuable than a failed push
- Use your results screen as a quick reality check
