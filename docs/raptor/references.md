## Egg-Related Progress Notes

### Common reminders

- Egg progress appears to matter for pass unlocks
- Missing progress may sometimes be tied to **room 20**
- Egg runs are much harder if you have not upgraded **egg health**
- Some players reported milestone reward issues

### Late-run recovery note

- If the egg breaks in **room 19** or **room 20**
- a player may be able to go back for another egg
- this only seems possible while **someone is still in room 20**

![Raptor Heist room 20 egg location](/img/raptor/eggs/raptor-egg-room-20-location-01.png)

## Daily Reward Systems

The two most consistently referenced reward systems were:

- **daily quest**
- **daily chest**

These were repeatedly suggested as a practical source of diamonds, especially for newer or weaker players trying to improve their egg setup.
