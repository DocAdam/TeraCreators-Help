# Raptor Heist Tasks

## Escaping With an Egg

Use this when you are trying to push egg-based progress or unlock egg-related pass milestones.

### Steps

1. Start the run and make sure you actually **pick up the egg**.
2. If your progress has seemed inconsistent before, pay close attention to **room 20**.
3. Before doing serious egg runs, make sure you have invested in **egg health** and other survival upgrades.
4. Protect the egg through the run instead of treating it like a normal clear.
5. Escape successfully with the egg.
6. After the run, check whether your egg total updated.

### What matters most

- Picking up the egg correctly
- Not missing room 20-related progression
- Having enough egg durability to finish the run

## Building a Stronger Early Run

Use this when your progression feels slow or unstable.

### Steps

1. Claim your **daily quest** rewards.
2. Claim your **daily chest** rewards.
3. Use those rewards to build up **diamonds**.
4. Spend those diamonds on upgrades that improve survival first.
5. Add **egg health** upgrades before forcing egg milestone runs.
6. If needed, do easier runs or take carries while building resources.
7. Retry harder progression goals once your build feels steadier.

### What this fixes

This helps with the most common early problem in the source: trying to push egg goals before your build is ready.

## Checking Why Egg Progress Looks Wrong

Use this when your egg total or milestone progress does not look right.

### Steps

1. Confirm that you actually picked up the egg during the run.
2. Check whether you may have missed the egg interaction in **room 20**.
3. Confirm that you finished the run the way the objective expects.
4. Compare your result against the pass milestones:
   - **30 eggs** for Velociraptor
   - **50 eggs** for Dilophosaurus
5. If progress still looks wrong, treat it as a possible progression or reward issue rather than assuming you did something wrong.

## Preparing for an Egg-Focused Progress Session

Use this when you want to dedicate runs specifically to egg progress.

### Steps

1. Make sure your basic survival upgrades are in decent shape.
2. Put diamonds into **egg health** if you have not already.
3. Claim daily rewards before starting.
4. Decide whether you are doing a serious push or just a few safer runs.
5. Watch room 20 carefully.
6. If the egg breaks late and the lobby state allows it, try the recovery trick.
7. Check your milestone total after each attempt so you notice issues early.
