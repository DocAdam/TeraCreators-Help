# Raptor Heist Tips

## Understanding Pass Unlocks

You do **not** appear to start with every pass active.

Community guidance pointed to this pass progression:

- **Legacy Pass** is the starting pass
- **Velociraptor** unlocks at **30 eggs**
- **Dilophosaurus** unlocks at **50 eggs**

If your egg count looks wrong, one recurring explanation was that a player may have missed the egg in **room 20**.

### Quick reference

- Starting pass: **Legacy**
- Unlock Velociraptor: **30 eggs**
- Unlock Dilophosaurus: **50 eggs**

## Prioritizing Early Progress

If your runs feel weak early on, the community advice was pretty consistent:

- claim your **daily quest**
- claim your **daily chest**
- use those rewards to build up **diamonds**
- spend upgrades on the things that help you survive
- do not over-focus on egg runs until your build is ready for them

The repeated point was that trying to force egg progress too early can waste runs if your defenses and upgrades are too weak.

### Good early priorities

1. **Survival**
2. **Egg health**
3. **Reliable daily rewards**
4. **Steady upgrades instead of spreading resources everywhere**

![Raptor Heist daily rewards screen](/img/raptor/menus/raptor-daily-rewards-screen-01.jpg)

## Upgrading Egg Durability Before Serious Egg Runs

A recurring warning from community replies was that escaping with an egg is much harder if you have **no egg health upgrades**.

Before pushing hard for egg milestones:

- improve your main survival upgrades
- improve **egg health**
- collect easy resources first
- use daily rewards to smooth out weak spots

![Raptor Heist egg upgrades screen](/img/raptor/menus/raptor-egg-upgrades-screen-01.jpg)

## Watching for Room 20 Egg Confusion

One recurring answer suggested that missing progress can sometimes be explained by a missed egg in **room 20**.

That makes room 20 important for two reasons:

- it may affect whether your egg progress counts the way you expect
- it also comes up in community discussion about recovering an egg late in a run

If your milestone progress seems off, room 20 is one of the first places to double-check.

![Raptor Heist room 20 egg area](/img/raptor/eggs/raptor-room-20-egg-area-01.png)

## Recovering an Egg Late in a Run

There is a community-reported recovery trick for late egg runs:

- if the egg breaks in **room 19** or the **raptor room 20**
- you may be able to go back and grab another egg
- this only works while **someone is still in room 20**

Treat this as a useful situational trick, not something to rely on every run.

![Raptor Heist egg recovery near room 20](/img/raptor/eggs/raptor-egg-recovery-room-20-01.jpg)

## Getting Carried Is Still a Valid Short-Term Strategy

If you are too weak to progress cleanly, getting carried for a while is still a workable short-term way to build resources.

That lines up with the rest of the guide:

- collect daily rewards
- stabilize your upgrades
- use easier or supported runs
- return to harder egg objectives once your build is stronger
