# Raptor Heist FAQ

## How do I unlock the other passes?

Community guidance pointed to this progression:

- **Legacy Pass** is the starting pass
- **Velociraptor** unlocks at **30 eggs**
- **Dilophosaurus** unlocks at **50 eggs**

If your total seems off, check whether you missed the egg in **room 20**.

![Raptor Heist pass screen](/img/raptor/passes/raptor-passes-screen-01.png)

---

## Why is my egg count not increasing?

The most common explanations were:

- you may not have actually collected the egg for that run
- you may have missed something in **room 20**
- your build may not be stable enough to finish the egg run cleanly
- there may also be a reward or progress issue in some cases

Safest order to check:

1. verify the run setup
2. double-check room 20
3. then consider the possibility of a bug or milestone issue

![Raptor Heist egg milestones screen](/img/raptor/eggs/raptor-egg-milestones-01.jpg)

## What should I upgrade first?

The advice leaned toward:

- **survival first**
- **egg health** if you want reliable egg progress
- **daily quest** and **daily chest** as steady resource sources

That is more useful than trying to spread upgrades evenly across everything.

![Raptor Heist progress menu](/img/raptor/menus/raptor-progress-menu-01.jpg)

---

## How do I get more diamonds?

The clearest recurring answers were:

- **daily quest**
- **daily chest**
- general progression through more successful runs

Players also referred others to codes as a useful source of rewards, especially when trying to afford egg-related upgrades.

![Raptor Heist daily chest screen](/img/raptor/menus/raptor-daily-chest-screen-01.jpg)

---

## Can I recover an egg if it breaks late?

One community-reported answer said yes, in some situations:

- if the egg breaks in **room 19** or **room 20**
- you may be able to go back and get another egg
- this only works while **someone is still in room 20**

Treat it as situational help, not a guaranteed system.

---

## How do I remove the loyal raptor?

Community replies said the loyal raptor was tied to a limited-time event and would stay until the event ended.

One message said that event would remain until **March 1, 2026**.

![Raptor Heist loyal raptor event screenshot](/img/raptor/events/raptor-loyal-raptor-event-01.jpg)

---

## Why did I not receive my egg milestone rewards?

There were reports of players hitting egg milestones without receiving all expected rewards automatically.

That means missing rewards were reported by players and may not always be caused by user error.
