# Raptor Heist Terms

## Carry

A run where a stronger player helps weaker players progress, survive, or earn rewards more easily.

---

## Daily Chest

A recurring reward source that players mentioned as a good way to collect diamonds and support early progression.

---

## Daily Quest

Another recurring reward source mentioned as a reliable way to build diamonds and fund upgrades.

---

## Dilophosaurus Pass

A pass the community said unlocks after escaping with **50 eggs**.

---

## Egg Health

Upgrades or durability related to keeping the egg intact during a run.

---

## Egg Milestone

A progression checkpoint tied to total eggs collected or escaped with.

Examples mentioned in community discussion included **30 eggs**, **35 eggs**, **40 eggs**, and **50 eggs**.

---

## Egg Run

A run where the goal is not just to clear or survive, but to **protect and escape with an egg** for progression.

---

## Escape

Successfully finishing the run in the way the game expects, especially when trying to count egg-related progress.

---

## Legacy Pass

The pass the community said players start with by default.

---

## Milestone Reward

A reward that should unlock after reaching a certain progression checkpoint, such as an egg total.

Some players reported cases where these rewards did not appear correctly.

---

## Pass

A progression track or progression category tied to advancement and unlocks.

In the source, players discussed **Legacy Pass**, **Velociraptor**, and **Dilophosaurus**.

---

## Room 20

A late-game room that came up repeatedly in community discussion.

It was mentioned in relation to:

- missing egg progress
- egg pickup confusion
- a situational late-run egg recovery trick

---

## Survival Upgrades

General upgrades that make it easier to stay alive and finish runs.

---

## Velociraptor Pass

A pass the community said unlocks after escaping with **30 eggs**.
