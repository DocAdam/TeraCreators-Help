# Havoc Hotel 1 FAQ

## Why are my weapons gone when I come back to HH1?

That is how HH1 currently works. Players reported that you need to buy your weapons again every time you rejoin a lobby.

### Important

- This is not described as a one-time unlock system
- Do not expect purchased weapons to remain available after leaving and rejoining

### Notes

- A community reply specifically confirmed this behavior

## What do I do when a room says the hotel is made of unbreakable material and to try breaking something?

One player later realized this was not a real puzzle clue. In their case, the door itself was bugged and they wasted time trying to break objects in the room.

### Important

- Do not assume every such message means there is a hidden environmental puzzle
- If the room will not open after a normal clear, it may be a broken door or room issue instead

### Notes

- This was reported as a one-off experience, so do not treat it as a standard intended mechanic
