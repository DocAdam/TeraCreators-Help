# Havoc Hotel 1 Tasks

## Searching for the Last Missing Guest

If you are stuck at 13 or 14 guests found, check only the rooms where you have not already found a guest.

### Steps

1. Review which rooms already gave you a guest.
2. Ignore rooms where you already found one in the current lobby.
3. Search the remaining rooms carefully.
4. If you are down to one missing guest, focus on the rooms you have not cleared for guests yet.

### Important

- Guest progress was described as lobby-based, not run-based
- If someone in the same lobby already got all 14 guests, you may not be able to collect them again there

### Notes

- One community reply said the final missing guest can be in one of several remaining rooms

## Submitting Leaderboard Runs

To get on the leaderboard, players were told to submit their result in the leaderboard channel and include proof.

### Steps

1. Finish your run or escape with your qualifying result.
2. Take a screenshot before leaving the game.
3. Submit the screenshot in the leaderboard submission channel.
4. Make sure the screenshot clearly shows the result you want counted.

### Important

- A player specifically said they had to wait because they forgot to take a screenshot before getting off
- Without proof, your result may be harder to verify

## Clearing Room 5 Safely

For the room with the first boss, players suggested baiting the first rocket, thinning enemies at the door, and then pushing in.

### Steps

1. Approach the room carefully and do not rush straight in.
2. Pick off the first enemies you can see near the entrance.
3. Bait out the boss’s first rocket.
4. After the first rocket is out, push forward.
5. Focus on headshots and controlled damage instead of panic rushing.
6. Use utility if available, such as nitro splash or a slo-mo device.

### Important

- This was shared as player strategy, not an official room guide
- The room was described as a bottleneck, so doorway control matters

### Notes

- The same player said they often run pistol, AR, nitro splash, and a slo-mo device

## Responding to a Door That Does Not Open

If a door does not open after clearing enemies, a community reply suggested respawning.

### Steps

1. Confirm all visible enemies are eliminated.
2. Check whether the room door still remains locked.
3. If the run appears stuck, try respawning.
4. Re-check whether the room state resets correctly after respawn.

### Important

- This is a workaround shared by another player
- It may help in a stuck room, but it was not presented as a guaranteed fix

### Notes

- Ignore this as a permanent mechanic
- It is better treated as troubleshooting guidance
