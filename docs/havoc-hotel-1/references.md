# Havoc Hotel 1 References

## Practical HH1 Community Notes

These are the most useful practical notes pulled from the reviewed HH1 help messages.

### Entries

- HH1 purchases are largely per lobby, not permanent unlocks
- Weapons usually need to be rebought after rejoining
- Coins, times, escapes, and diamonds were described as the main persistent stats
- HH1 appears to have 14 guests, even when the counter sometimes shows 18
- Guest collection appears to be lobby-based
- Private lobbies can make farming and guest hunting easier
- Room 5 strategy shared by players: bait the first rocket, clear the entrance, then push in
- A stuck room door may sometimes be solved by respawning
- No confirmed HH1 code list was found in this source

### Important

- Some items here are community-reported and may still need official confirmation
- Transitory bug reports and one-off complaints were intentionally not expanded into guide sections

### Notes

- This guide focuses on repeated, practical help rather than unresolved support issues
