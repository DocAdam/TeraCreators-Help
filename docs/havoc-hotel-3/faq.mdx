# FAQ

## What does prestige reset, and what does it keep?

<Term name="Prestige">Prestige</Term> resets your coins, cash on hand, and power-up progression. <Term name="Soap">Soap</Term> already invested in weapon classes stays invested, but the weapon classes lock again after prestiging. <Term name="Prestige">Prestige</Term> also improves long-term progression by increasing certain rewards over time.

### Important

- Invested soap stays invested
- Weapon classes relock after prestige
- Early prestige runs may feel weak at first

### Notes

- Community discussion also mentions that prestige scaling continues across many prestige levels

---

## How does AFK income work?

<Term name="AFK">AFK</Term> income is based on how much money you currently have on hand. The more money you have, the more AFK income you earn, up to the cap allowed by your coin reward booth upgrades. The gold coins in the lobby are just XP and do not determine AFK payout.

### Important

- AFK income has a cap
- Your current money total affects the rate
- Coin booth upgrades affect the limit

### Notes

- Reported cap values in chat appear to be community-reported, so avoid locking in a hard number unless you verify it elsewhere

---

## What does delisting do?

Delisting removes that rarity from your <Term name="Loot pool">loot pool</Term>. Listing adds it back. In practice, if a rarity shows **Delist**, it is currently active. If it shows **List**, it is currently inactive.

### Important

- **Delist = active**
- **List = inactive**
- At least one rarity must remain active in an opened category

### Notes

- This applies to both weapon classes and booster rewards

## How many rescues are there?

The reported total is **20** rescues. Players also report that duplicate rescues can push the displayed count higher, including counts like **35/20**.

### Important

- The base total is still treated as **20**
- Duplicate rescues change the visible count, not the reported base total

## What is the best way to make money early?

The most common advice is to start with <Term name="Rescue">rescues</Term>, daily rewards, and any seasonal currency sources you can gather. Once you can survive deeper into the map, move into <Term name="Killstreak">killstreak</Term> <Term name="Farming">farming</Term> and later-room <Term name="Farming">farming</Term> routes.

### Important

- Rescues are strong early because their value scales
- Later-room farming becomes better once survival is reliable
