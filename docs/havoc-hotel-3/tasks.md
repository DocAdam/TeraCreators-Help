# Tasks

## Managing Weapon Rarity Listings

Use listing and delisting to control which weapon rarities or booster rarities can appear in your loot pool.

### Steps

1. Open the weapon class or booster category you want to manage.
2. Invest soap until the rarities you want are unlocked.
3. Leave the rarities you want active showing **Delist**.
4. Change the rarities you do not want to **List** so they stop appearing.
5. Keep at least one rarity active in any opened class or category.

### Important

- **List = inactive**
- **Delist = active**
- Once a class is opened, at least one rarity must remain active
- The same system applies to booster rewards

### Notes

- Example: if you only want Exotic rewards, Exotic should show **Delist** and lower rarities should show **List**

## Setting Up a Killstreak Farming Route

This route is one of the most repeated recommendations for making money quickly.

### Steps

1. Spend 1 diamond on the **unbreakable killstreak** booster.
2. Enter the run and build a killstreak in **room 2** and **room 13**.
3. Continue forward once your multiplier is built.
4. Farm the **samurai boss in room 25** or the **green aliens in room 29**.
5. Repeat the route as needed.

### Important

- This works best if you can reliably survive to those rooms
- AFK lobby multipliers can make this route much stronger

### Notes

- This is one of the clearest repeated farming methods in the guide

## Double Rescuing Hostages

You can repeat most rescues to increase your rescue total and take better advantage of the rescue multiplier.

### Steps

1. Rescue the hostage normally.
2. Immediately accept the room reward.
3. Return to the hostage location.
4. Rescue the same hostage a second time.

### Important

- This reportedly works on **almost all** rescues, but not all of them
- Players report that this can increase the count up to **35/20** even though the guide count says **20** rescues total

### Notes

- This is community-reported and should be labeled that way in final documentation

[Example image: before-and-after screenshots of the rescue counter showing the count increasing beyond the normal total, such as 20/20 and then 35/20.]

## Timing Weapon Unlocks Before Prestige

You can reduce unwanted loot pool clutter after prestiging by delaying some unlocks.

### Steps

1. Open and use only the weapon classes you want during the current prestige cycle.
2. Max other systems first.
3. Wait until just before prestiging to unlock the remaining weapon classes.
4. Prestige after those unlocks are complete.
5. After prestige, the classes relock, which helps keep the early loot pool cleaner again.

### Important

- Invested soap stays invested
- Relocking does not erase the soap already spent

### Notes

- This is a progression strategy based on player advice
