# Havoc Hotel 2 FAQ

## What does prestige do in HH2?

Community guidance says prestiging is mostly for bragging rights in HH2.

It resets progression in ways that raise prices again, but the shared discussion did not include exact numeric increases for shop prices, hostage rescue multipliers, or elimination streak multipliers.

### Important

- Exact price increase values were not confirmed in the shared resource
- A community reply specifically warned that prestiging is mostly for bragging rights
- Players were advised to check what still benefits from prestige before doing it

### Notes

- If you want a fully verified prestige breakdown, this section needs either direct in-game testing or a developer-confirmed source

## How do I upgrade the crown?

The clearest community answer says to max out everything.

One detailed reply also describes crown progression as moving from Wood to Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Ruby.

### Important

- Crown progression reported by the community:
  - Wood
  - Bronze
  - Silver
  - Gold
  - Diamond
  - Ruby
- One community reply also claimed there are six levels per crown stage

### Notes

- This looks community-reported rather than officially documented

## Is the Stormstriker only a one-time use item?

A community reply said yes, as far as they could tell.

Another later question about getting Swarmstrike again after dying did not add a clearer correction.

### Important

- Treat Swarmstrike as single-use unless you verify otherwise in-game

- This section is based on player experience, not an official game rule post
