## Understanding Max Upgrade Levels

Community replies provided a rough breakdown of max levels for several HH2 upgrade categories.

### Entries

- **Coin level-ups**
  - Up to 100 for each tab
- **Weapons**
  - Believed to have 5 tiers each
- **Personal power-ups**
  - Damage: max 4
  - Health regen: max 10
  - Shield regen: max 10
  - Health: up to 200
  - Shield: up to 200
- **Most effect power-ups**
  - Max 3
- **Crowns**
  - Progress from Wood to Ruby

### Important

- This is community-reported and should be treated as practical guidance, not official patch-note documentation
