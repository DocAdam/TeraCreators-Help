# Havoc Hotel 2 Tasks

## Claiming Room-Clear Weapons

If you clear a room and cannot figure out how to pick up the weapon reward, use your harvesting tool.

### Steps

1. Clear the room.
2. Switch to your harvesting tool.
3. Use it to interact with the reward pickup.

- This answer came from a direct player help exchange
